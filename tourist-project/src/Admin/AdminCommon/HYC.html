About Us

HackYourFuture is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds with the skills needed to enter the tech industry. Our mission is to empower those who face significant barriers to employment by offering them a comprehensive web development education. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to succeed, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our program is designed to bridge the gap between potential and opportunity, creating a pathway to meaningful employment.


Our team consists of passionate volunteers and professional developers who share a common goal: to make a lasting impact on the lives of our students. By fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment, we ensure that each trainee receives the guidance and encouragement needed to thrive. We take pride in seeing our graduates succeed in the workforce, knowing all the hard work and dedication that plays a part in their journey towards a better future.


At HackYourFuture, diversity and inclusion are at the heart of everything we do. We embrace the unique perspectives that each student brings to the table, and we are committed to creating a community where everyone feels valued. We create a space where people enjoy being and it in up to all involved in the community to bring their ideas, contributions and effort together and share this with the group. Each person plays an important role in shaping the HackYourFuture journey.
